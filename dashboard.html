🎯 This Week's Focus
Reduce Gum Inflammation
Floss gently every evening and use an alcohol-free rinse. Cold water rinses soothe tender gum tissue between brushing.
💡 Rinse with cold water after meals to calm gum tissue
📖 Week 18 — What's Happening
Elevated progesterone increases blood flow to gum tissue — causing tenderness and reactivity. This is the ideal window for a dental cleaning if you haven't had one yet.

Did You Know
Fluoride helps baby's teeth too
Trace fluoride from your toothpaste supports your baby's developing enamel in utero. It's one more reason brushing twice daily matters.
Your baby's teeth form starting at week 6
