About the Program

What is the SmileMom Externship?

SmileMom is a student-built oral health platform connecting dentistry to medicine — across pregnancy, chronic disease, military health, and more. We're expanding rapidly, and we need market researchers to help us understand who we serve and where the biggest gaps are.

This externship gives you the rare opportunity to work on real projects with real impact. Your research will directly shape which communities SmileMom builds for next — whether that's veterans, children, cancer patients, or underserved populations.

You'll be supported by a dedicated program manager, weekly Zoom check-ins, a Slack community with fellow externs, and access to SmileMom's full knowledge base. At the end, your name will be credited on the SmileMom platform and you'll receive a certificate of completion.

This externship is unpaid and fully remote — designed to fit your schedule while giving you the professional experience, mentorship, and portfolio work that sets you apart in dental school, medical school, public health, and business applications.

What you'll actually do

Research underserved communities with significant oral health gaps
Survey and interview target patient populations
Analyze existing oral health resources and identify what's missing
Write market research reports on specific medical specialties or communities
Present findings at weekly team meetings
Contribute to SmileMom's expansion roadmap and content strategy
Review and fact-check health content against primary sources (CDC, ADA, NIH)
Help identify partnership opportunities with clinics, schools, and organizations
Research Tracks

Choose your focus area

Every extern is assigned to a research track based on their background and interests. Each track produces a real deliverable that goes directly into SmileMom's development pipeline.

🏥

Medical Specialty Integration

Research how oral health connects to a specific medical specialty — cardiology, neurology, rheumatology, oncology, or others. Identify what doctors in that field should know and what resources are missing.

Deliverable: Specialty research report + page contentFor: Pre-dental, pre-med, public health students
🌍

Underserved Community Research

Identify communities with the greatest oral health gaps — veterans, homeless populations, rural communities, immigrant communities, low-income families — and document what resources exist and what's missing.

Deliverable: Community needs assessment reportFor: Public health, social work, pre-dental students
📱

Digital Health & User Research

Survey real users about their oral health habits, barriers to care, and what digital tools they'd find useful. Analyze the competitive landscape of oral health apps and platforms.

Deliverable: User research report + competitive analysisFor: Business, marketing, UX design students
🎓

Educational Content Research

Research what patients with specific conditions — diabetes, pregnancy, cancer, eating disorders — actually need to know about their oral health, and verify all content against primary clinical sources.

Deliverable: Fact-checked content brief + source citationsFor: Pre-dental, nursing, health communication students
🪖

Military & Veteran Health

Research the oral health needs of active duty service members and veterans — readiness classifications, VA dental gaps, PTSD connections, and what resources currently exist through the military healthcare system.

Deliverable: Military oral health gap analysisFor: Veterans, ROTC students, pre-med/pre-dental
🤝

Partnership & Outreach Research

Identify potential partner organizations — dental schools, community health centers, hospital systems, nonprofits — that align with SmileMom's mission. Research their programs and draft outreach strategies.

Deliverable: Partnership landscape report + outreach templatesFor: Business, communications, public health students

Program Timeline

Four weeks. Real deliverables.

Each cohort runs for 4 weeks with structured milestones, flexible hours, and full support from your program manager.

1
Week 1

Orientation & Scoping

Meet your team, choose your track, and define your research scope. Complete your profile and attend the onboarding Zoom.

2
Week 2

Primary Research

Deep dive into your topic. Survey and interview target populations. Gather data from primary sources including CDC, ADA, NIH, and peer-reviewed literature.

3
Week 3

Analysis & Writing

Synthesize your research into a structured report. Weekly check-in with your program manager. Draft your key findings and recommendations.

4
Week 4

Presentation & Handoff

Present your findings to the SmileMom team. Final deliverable submission. Receive your certificate and recommendation eligibility assessment.
